A short walkthrough of who you are and how you work. Seven steps. About four minutes if you rush. If you sit with it, you'll have a card just like this one, but yours, with your name on it.

Print the booklet. Share the card with your team. Keep it for yourself. It's designed to start the conversation with the most important person in the room, which is you.

Want your team to do this too? Copy a blank link and send it around. Everyone ends up with their own filled-in card, and you see each other for who you actually are.

The share moment

Ready to share?

Your handbook is saved. The card below is the human snapshot of how you work best. The more places it lands, the easier it becomes for the people around you to see who you actually are.

Your share card is the image for LinkedIn, Slack, email, or wherever your people are. Post it to start a conversation about who you are and how you work best. The PDF booklet is the A4 keepsake with all the detail, ideal for a one-to-one with your manager or quietly kept for yourself. Email a copy sends the card and the booklet to you, a manager or a colleague.
